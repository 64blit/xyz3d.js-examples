I design and build websites, games, apps, tools, plugins, and art. With the aim to support free education initiatives and provide support to those who need it most.

Science, technology, and art has fascinated me from a young age. That curiosity led me into an undergraduates program of Computer Science, where I worked as a department tutor and web freelancer. My free time was quickly engulfed with coding and app design. It was around this time that I realized my software hobby had turned into a passion. Out of university, I joined a small team working on holographic displays as a Software Engineer. There I focused on interactive 3D demos, contributing to their content creation pipelines, providing developer support, and a handful of other things. Since then, I’ve ventured into unknown territory; Discovering what it means to start a game studio, with a team and without, studied economics, design, art, crypto, education, and greatly grown my skills as a software creative.
